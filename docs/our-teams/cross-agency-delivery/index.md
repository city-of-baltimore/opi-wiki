# Cross-Agency Delivery

> The service overlay OPI uses for problems that cross agency boundaries — and how that work moves from inputs to outcomes.

Cross-Agency Delivery is not a separate team, portfolio, or cost center. It is the service overlay OPI activates when a priority outcome requires multiple agencies to move together. The [Innovation Lab](../innovation-lab/index.md) provides the craft when discovery, design, prototyping, or product work is needed. [Performance](../performance-and-citistat/index.md) provides the executive performance routine that can surface the problem and monitor the gain after sustainment. AdminOps keeps the work visible, documented, and ready for handoff.

Read this section alongside [How Work Moves Through OPI](../../how-we-work/how-work-moves-through-opi.md), which explains the signal-to-solution loop across all five OPI services.
