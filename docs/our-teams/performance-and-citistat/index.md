# Performance

> Citywide performance management and the CitiStat methodology.

This is the Performance section of the OPI wiki. It is the home of Baltimore’s executive performance rhythm: how priorities become measures, how leaders see what is working and what is stuck, and how commitments turn into measurable service improvements.

A Stat is the right routine when a recurring performance problem needs leadership attention, reliable-enough data, commitments, follow-up, and a closure mechanism. A Stat may trigger a tiger team, product discovery, a data quality sprint, a Cross-Agency Delivery activation, or a one-time executive decision. The goal is not more meetings; it is clearer ownership, faster decisions, and measurable improvements.

## New to CitiStat? Start here

**Public reader (Council, agency partner, resident).** Start with **About Performance** for the plain-language overview of the service, then **CitiStat Strategic Framework** for the strategic case and the active portfolio. Browse the **CitiStat Portfolio** to see specific Stats.

**OPI staff running or supporting a Stat.** Start with **About Performance** for shared language, then go straight to the **CitiStat Method Playbook** for the operating cycle and admission criteria. Pull artifact templates from **CitiStat Templates**, hold every artifact to the **CitiStat Quality Standards**, and keep the **Staff Quick Reference** open during sessions.

**Deputy Mayor teams, City Administrator’s Office, agency Data-Driven Officers.** Read **About Performance**, the **Method Playbook** (especially admission criteria, follow-up, and escalation), and the brief for the Stats you participate in.
