# Performance

> The team that runs citywide performance management and supports the CitiStat program.

This is the Performance section of the OPI wiki. The Performance team owns Baltimore's executive performance rhythm: how priorities become measures, how leaders see what is working and what is stuck, and how commitments turn into measurable service improvements.

Performance management is the team and its service. **[CitiStat](../../programs/citistat/index.md) is a program** — the recurring, citywide routine the team operates day to day, supported by all of OPI. The program's method, templates, quality bar, and agency portfolio now live in their own [CitiStat program section](../../programs/citistat/index.md). This page stays focused on the team and the service it delivers.

A Stat is the right routine when a recurring performance problem needs leadership attention, reliable-enough data, commitments, follow-up, and a closure mechanism. A Stat may trigger a tiger team, product discovery, a data quality sprint, a Cross-Agency Delivery activation, or a one-time executive decision. The goal is not more meetings; it is clearer ownership, faster decisions, and measurable improvements.

## The CitiStat program

The Performance team is the operational home of [CitiStat](../../programs/citistat/index.md), the program supported by all OPI teams. Start there for the strategic framework, the method playbook, the staff quick reference, quality standards, templates, and the full agency portfolio.

## Works closely with

The team works closely with [Cross-Agency Delivery](../../how-we-work/services/cross-agency-delivery.md) — the service OPI activates for problems that span agency boundaries. Performance owns the standing delivery reviews and embedded performance routines that sustain cross-agency work after a tiger team finishes.
