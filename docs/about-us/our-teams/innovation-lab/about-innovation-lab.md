## PURPOSE

### What this service is for

The Innovation Lab turns service problems into tested, adopted solutions. It is OPI's service-improvement and applied-innovation capability — the craft of solving — used to make city services easier to access, track, and complete.

### Who we are

A small, multidisciplinary team: civic designers, an applied data scientist, a full-stack engineer, and an Innovation Program Manager. We design *with* frontline staff and residents, not just for them.

### What we do

Discovery, service design, user research, product management, prototyping, civic technology, applied AI pilots, and MVP-and-handoff. A typical engagement: understand how a service actually works, design with the people closest to it, prototype a fix in real conditions, and hand a working improvement to the agency that will run it.

### Why it matters

Service problems usually look like technology, policy, or staffing problems in isolation — they are usually all three. Without a team that can hold the whole problem and design across boundaries, agencies cannot move from analysis to action.

### How we work

Time-boxed, evidence-led, and adoption-first. We design for handoff from day one, test before we scale, and reach for emerging methods like AI only when they solve a real operational problem.

### What we own

- Human-centered and service design
- Product discovery and product management
- Resident and staff engagement and research
- Prototypes, pilots, and in-house digital products
- Operational workflow tools and data-collection systems
- Responsible AI pilots focused on service improvement
- The Public Innovation Toolkit and the Lab's methods

### What we do not own

- Agency operations — the agency owns the workflow after handoff
- Long-term operation of the tools we build — handed off with a sustainment plan
- Cross-agency coordination authority — that is [Cross-Agency Delivery](../../../what-we-do/services/cross-agency-delivery.md)

### How we partner across OPI

CitiStat surfaces the problem; Data and Analytics provides the evidence and platform; Cross-Agency Delivery coordinates when multiple agencies must move together; AdminOps sustains the gain.

### What success looks like

Residents can access, track, and complete services more easily; agencies own the improvements after the Lab steps back; a small portfolio of credible, visible wins ships each year.
