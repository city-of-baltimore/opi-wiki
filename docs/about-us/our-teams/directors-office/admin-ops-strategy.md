# AdminOps Strategy

{{ page_header(summary="OPI's strategy for the AdminOps service — the operating backbone.", category="STRATEGY", tagline="The internal operating system that lets OPI's four citywide services produce their best work.") }}

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

AdminOps is the service the Director's Office delivers to keep OPI's work coordinated, documented, resourced, and clearly communicated. A team that asks city agencies to operate with discipline must hold itself to the same standard. AdminOps is how OPI does that.

## SERVICE DEFINITION

AdminOps owns the administrative, operational, communications, and portfolio-management functions that support OPI: intake, project management, executive briefings, communications and brand, Council relations, knowledge management, fiscal and procurement coordination, HR and facilities, quality assurance for public-facing materials, and the systems and standards that keep delivery consistent.

## WHY THIS WORK MATTERS

Without strong intake, project management, communications, and people operations, a small office becomes reactive and personality-dependent. AdminOps is the operating system beneath the other four services. It is the difference between a team that ships reliably and one that runs on heroics.

## WHAT THE SERVICE OWNS

- The single front-door intake and triage process
- Portfolio visibility and the Book of Business
- Project-management coordination and the project-gate process
- Communications, brand, and Council readiness
- Knowledge management and the OPI wiki
- Fiscal, procurement, HR, and facilities coordination
- Quality assurance for public-facing materials

## WHAT THE SERVICE DOES NOT OWN

- The substance of the four citywide services. AdminOps supports them; it does not deliver them
- Agency operations

## OPERATING POSTURE

Documented over heroic. Processes that survive leadership transitions. A single front door beats freelance project starts. Investment in OPI's own operations returns time to the citywide services.

## CORE OFFERINGS

- Intake and chartering
- Portfolio tracking and reporting
- Executive briefings and Council coordination
- Communications and knowledge management
- Quality assurance and operating standards

## HOW THE SERVICE PARTNERS ACROSS OPI

### With Performance

Council posture and public narrative for CitiStat; portfolio visibility on commitments.

### With Data and Analytics

Communications and QA for public data products; knowledge-management standards.

### With the Innovation Lab

Intake and portfolio tracking for Lab projects; communications for launches.

### With Cross-Agency Delivery

Portfolio visibility, narrative, and Council briefings for activations.
