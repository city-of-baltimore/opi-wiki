## How proposed changes are reviewed

Every change goes through the same path:

1. **Proposal** — a pull request, issue, or email comes in.
2. **Triage** — the wiki maintainer reviews within five business days.
3. **Owner review** — the relevant content owner (e.g. the Deputy CDO for a Data and Analytics page) reviews for accuracy.
4. **Merge** — the change ships to the live site automatically via GitHub Actions.

## Editorial voice

OPI Foundations is written in plain language. We avoid jargon where we can, define it where we can't, and prefer concrete nouns and active verbs. When in doubt, write the way you'd explain something to a thoughtful friend who doesn't work in city government.

## What lives where

This is the public reference. If you're looking for an internal SOP, a personnel record, or a vendor contract, that lives elsewhere — in SharePoint, in HR systems, or in procurement records — and not in this wiki.
