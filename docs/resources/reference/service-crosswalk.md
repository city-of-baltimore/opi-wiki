# Service Crosswalk — Mandate to Outcomes

{{ page_header(summary="One scannable view of OPI's five services: cost center, legal mandate, goal, and the outcomes each one buys.", category="OPI · ACCOUNTABILITY") }}

This page maps each budgeted service to the City Code mandate behind it, the goal it pursues, and the outcomes it is accountable for — so oversight can see how OPI's structure maps to public value. Per-service activity detail lives on each [service page](../../what-we-do/services/index.md); the FY priorities and budget figures live in the [Strategic Priorities one-pager](strategic-priorities-one-pager.md).

| Service | Delivered by | Mandate (Baltimore City Code) | Goal | Outcomes it is accountable for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Citywide Performance Management](../../what-we-do/services/citywide-performance-management.md) | Performance team (runs CitiStat) | Article 1, Subtitle 61 (CitiStat) | Use timely, accurate information to improve service delivery and address problems before they become chronic | Problems addressed before chronic; commitments kept with evidence; performance visible without spreadsheets |
| [Citywide Data and Analytics](../../what-we-do/services/citywide-data-and-analytics.md) | Data and Analytics team | Article 1, Subtitle 9 (Open Data) | Make City data accurate, reliable, accessible, and useful for decisions and transparency | Decisions on trusted, current data; public data that's clear and current; one source of truth; AI readiness |
| [Innovation Lab](../../what-we-do/services/innovation-lab.md) | Innovation Lab team | Article 1, Subtitle 61 (Innovation Lab / continuous improvement) | Design, test, and implement practical solutions that make services easier to use and manage | Easier resident access/tracking; agencies own improvements after handoff; a few visible wins each year |
| [Cross-Agency Delivery](../../what-we-do/services/cross-agency-delivery.md) | Activated across teams (no standing team) | Operationally required by Subtitles 61 and 9 | Resolve cross-agency barriers, clarify ownership, and deliver what no single department can alone | Cross-agency issues resolved; clear ownership and sequencing; progress without new task forces; durable sustainment |
| [AdminOps](../../what-we-do/services/admin-ops.md) | Director's Office | Operationally required by Subtitles 61 and 9 | Keep OPI's work coordinated, documented, resourced, and clearly communicated | Priority work driven and tracked; clear partner timelines; commitments delivered and transparent |
