# Service Crosswalk: Mandate to Outcomes

{{ page_header(summary="One scannable view of OPI's five services: who delivers them, their legal mandate, their goal, and their outcomes.", category="OPI · ACCOUNTABILITY") }}

This page maps each service to its City Code mandate, the goal it pursues, and
the outcomes it is accountable for, so oversight can see how OPI's structure
delivers public value. Per-service activity detail lives on each
[service page](../../what-we-do/services/index.md).

| Service | Delivered by | Mandate (Baltimore City Code) | Goal | Outcomes it is accountable for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Citywide Performance Management](../../what-we-do/services/citywide-performance-management.md) | Performance team (runs CitiStat) | Article 1, Subtitle 61 (CitiStat) | Use timely, accurate information to improve service delivery and address problems before they become chronic | Problems addressed before chronic; commitments kept with evidence; performance visible without spreadsheets |
| [Citywide Data and Analytics](../../what-we-do/services/citywide-data-and-analytics.md) | Data and Analytics team | Article 1, Subtitle 9 (Open Data) | Make city data accurate, reliable, accessible, and useful for decisions and transparency | Decisions on trusted, current data; public data that's clear and current; one official record per dataset; AI readiness |
| [Innovation Lab](../../what-we-do/services/innovation-lab.md) | Innovation Lab | Article 1, Subtitle 61 (Innovation Lab / continuous improvement) | Design, test, and implement practical solutions that make services easier to use and manage | Easier resident access/tracking; agencies own improvements after handoff; a few visible wins each year |
| [Cross-Agency Delivery](../../what-we-do/services/cross-agency-delivery/index.md) | Activated across teams (no standing team) | Operationally required by Subtitles 61 and 9 | Resolve cross-agency barriers, clarify ownership, and deliver what no single department can alone | Cross-agency issues resolved; clear ownership and sequencing; progress without new task forces; durable sustainment |
| [AdminOps](../../what-we-do/services/admin-ops.md) | Director's Office | Operationally required by Subtitles 61 and 9 | Keep OPI's work coordinated, documented, resourced, and clearly communicated | Priority work driven and tracked; clear partner timelines; commitments delivered and transparent |
