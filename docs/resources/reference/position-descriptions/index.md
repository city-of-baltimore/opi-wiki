**■ How to use this index**

OPI maintains a position description (PD) for every role on the team. PDs are the canonical job documents — they are what we use for hiring, classification, performance management, and onboarding. This index is the directory: it tells you what roles exist, where they sit, who they report to, the City classification, and where to find the full PD.

> *PDs live in SharePoint under “09 · People and Roles → Position Descriptions.” The Chief of Staff and the relevant portfolio lead approve any change before it takes effect.*

**Conventions**

- Title format: Working Title (City Classification).

- Reports to: the position the role reports to in the OPI Operating Model.

- Status: “Filled,” “Open,” or “Contracted” — maintained against the personnel roster.

**■ Innovation Lab**

OPI’s Innovation Lab capacity is currently staffed through positions held within the Director’s Office (Civic Design and UX Lead, Project Manager) and Data and Analytics (Senior Product Manager, Technical Program Manager, engineers) on a portfolio basis. Dedicated Innovation Lab positions are slated for the FY27 staffing plan; they will be added to this index as they are approved and posted.

> *The Innovation Lab is a service-led portfolio with a method (Public Innovation Toolkit) and a pattern (six-month tiger teams). Until dedicated PDs are added, Lab work draws from the existing roles above on a project basis through the OPI intake process.*

**■ How this index is maintained**

**Owner.** The Chief of Staff is the owner of the PD index and the source of truth for working titles, reporting lines, and status.

**Source of truth.** Each PD .docx in SharePoint is canonical. This index summarizes; the PD governs.

**Change control.** Material changes (title, classification, reporting line, scope) require Chief of Staff sign-off and HR partner review.

**Review cadence.** Quarterly review by the Chief of Staff with portfolio leads. Annual sweep aligned to the budget cycle.

*Owned by the Chief of Staff with HR partner. Reviewed quarterly. Update whenever a position is posted, filled, vacated, or reclassified.*
