# Products

> The tools and platforms OPI builds and supports for the City.

A **product** is a tool, platform, or public-facing resource OPI builds or maintains. Products are distinct from services (what OPI delivers) and programs (ongoing routines): a product is a lasting thing with a roadmap, an owner, and users. Products often draw on more than one team: the [Innovation Lab](../../about-us/our-teams/innovation-lab/index.md) for design and build, and [Data and Analytics](../../about-us/our-teams/data-and-analytics/index.md) for the data layer.

| --- | --- | --- |
