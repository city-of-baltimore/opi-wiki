## What it is

The Baltimore City Data Platform is the shared data infrastructure — warehouse, pipelines, governance, and catalog — that OPI's [Data and Analytics](../../our-teams/data-and-analytics/index.md) team builds and operates. It is the data layer beneath OPI's dashboards, analytics products, and the [Baltimore Intelligence Center](baltimore-intelligence-center/index.md).
