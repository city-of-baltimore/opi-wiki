# Services

> What OPI delivers for the City: five services, delivered by four teams.

A **service** is what OPI delivers. It is distinct from a **team** (a group with staff and budget) and a **program** (an ongoing routine). Each of OPI's four teams delivers a service. Cross-Agency Delivery is delivered across teams, which makes five services in all.

| Service | What OPI delivers | Delivered by |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [AdminOps](admin-ops.md) | Keeps OPI running, documented, and sustained | Director's Office |
| [Citywide Performance Management](citywide-performance-management.md) | Turns priorities into measures and commitments through regular performance reviews | Performance team |
| [Citywide Data and Analytics](citywide-data-and-analytics.md) | Data and analytics the City can rely on to act | Data and Analytics team |
| [Innovation Lab](innovation-lab.md) | Design, product, and delivery methods that solve service problems | Innovation Lab |
| [Cross-Agency Delivery](cross-agency-delivery/index.md) | Coordination when a problem requires multiple agencies to act together | Drawn from across all of OPI; no standing team |

## How services relate to teams, programs, and products

Teams have staff and budget; services are what those teams deliver. [CitiStat](../programs/citistat/index.md) is a program, a routine that the Performance team operates and all teams support. Products like the [Baltimore City Data Platform](../products/index.md) are the tools and platforms OPI builds while delivering these services.
