### Public Purpose

Water Stat focuses on the utility operations that provide safe, reliable water and wastewater services for Baltimore. This is separate from Solid Waste because the operating model, infrastructure, regulation, and risk profile are different.

### Staff Purpose

Staff use Water Stat to review water main breaks, sewer overflows, water-in-cellar calls, wastewater treatment, billing-related operational issues, and compliance risks.

### Core Management Questions

- Are water and wastewater services reliable, safe, and responsive?
- Where are leaks, breaks, overflows, or water-in-cellar issues recurring?
- Which infrastructure, billing, or capital coordination issues require executive attention?
- What data or process gaps limit utility performance management?

### Starter Focus Areas

- Water main breaks, leaks, and response performance
- Sewer overflows, water-in-cellar calls, and wastewater treatment indicators
- Utility billing and infrastructure coordination issues

### Standard Artifacts and Data Products

- Pre-meeting memo, Stat deck, follow-up memo, and follow-up tracker.
- Core data product: Water and wastewater utility performance dashboard.

### Tiger Team / Delivery Triggers

- Utility issue requiring coordination with Finance, DGS, DOT, Law, or capital partners
- Recurring service issue requiring field validation or data quality sprint
- Infrastructure risk requiring executive action
