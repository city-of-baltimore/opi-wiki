### Public Purpose

Downtown Stat focuses on the health, safety, cleanliness, economic vitality, and public realm conditions of Downtown Baltimore. Downtown is a civic, employment, and business center; its performance affects residents, workers, visitors, and the city’s reputation.

### Staff Purpose

Staff use Downtown Stat to coordinate implementation of Downtown RISE priorities, track public safety and cleanliness conditions, support tenant retention and business activity, align agency work, and resolve place-based barriers.

### Core Management Questions

- Are Downtown RISE priorities moving from plan to implementation?
- Which blocks, corridors, or assets need coordinated action?
- How are safety, cleanliness, economic activity, and public realm conditions changing?
- What decisions are needed from leadership or partners to keep momentum?

### Starter Focus Areas

- Downtown RISE action tracking
- Block-level service demand and public realm conditions
- Tenant retention, economic vitality, and partner coordination

### Standard Artifacts and Data Products

- Pre-meeting memo, Stat deck, follow-up memo, and follow-up tracker.
- Core data product: Downtown RISE performance dashboard.

### Tiger Team / Delivery Triggers

- Downtown action item stalled across agencies
- Corridor or block condition requiring coordinated city response
- Partner or resource issue requiring executive authorizer decision
