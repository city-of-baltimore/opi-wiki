### Public Purpose

HR and Labor Stat focuses on the people systems that allow City government to hire, retain, support, and manage its workforce. Strong HR and labor operations affect every agency’s ability to deliver services.

### Staff Purpose

Staff use HR and Labor Stat to review hiring timelines, vacancy pipelines, classification issues, labor relations, workforce planning, and barriers that slow agency staffing.

### Core Management Questions

- Are agencies able to hire and onboard staff quickly enough to deliver services?
- Where are requisitions, classifications, labor processes, or onboarding steps slowing hiring?
- Which workforce risks affect priority services?
- How can central HR and Labor processes better support agency operations?

### Starter Focus Areas

- Vacancy and time-to-fill pipeline
- Classification, requisition, and onboarding bottlenecks
- Labor and workforce planning issues affecting service delivery

### Standard Artifacts and Data Products

- Pre-meeting memo, Stat deck, follow-up memo, and follow-up tracker.
- Core data product: Workforce and hiring pipeline dashboard.

### Tiger Team / Delivery Triggers

- Hiring or labor bottleneck affecting multiple agencies or priority service delivery
- Workforce process requiring Workday or policy changes
- Agency staffing issue requiring executive sponsor intervention
