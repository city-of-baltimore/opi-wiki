### Public Purpose

Rec Stat focuses on recreation centers, parks, forestry, senior programming, youth sports, therapeutic recreation, and public spaces that shape neighborhood quality of life.

### Staff Purpose

Staff use Rec Stat to monitor recreation participation, program capacity, forestry backlogs, facility maintenance, safety and security conditions, and capital or operational needs affecting parks and recreation services.

### Core Management Questions

- Are recreation programs and facilities accessible, safe, and well-used?
- Where are forestry, facility, or maintenance backlogs affecting neighborhoods?
- Are priority populations being served by recreation and parks programming?
- What operational or capital barriers need executive attention?

### Starter Focus Areas

- Forestry backlog and service request performance
- Recreation participation and program capacity
- Facility maintenance, safety, and staffing readiness

### Standard Artifacts and Data Products

- Pre-meeting memo, Stat deck, follow-up memo, and follow-up tracker.
- Core data product: Recreation, parks, and forestry performance dashboard.

### Tiger Team / Delivery Triggers

- Forestry or facility backlog requiring tiger team support
- Safety or security issue requiring interagency coordination
- Program capacity or staffing issue affecting service access
