### Public Purpose

Finance Stat focuses on the financial systems and processes that allow Baltimore City to steward public dollars, collect revenue, pay vendors, procure goods and services, manage grants, and support accountable government operations.

### Staff Purpose

Staff use Finance Stat to improve procurement, collections, payment systems, revenue operations, audit findings, contract data, M/WBE reporting, grant compliance, and financial service delivery to agencies and residents.

### Core Management Questions

- Are financial operations helping agencies deliver or creating avoidable delays?
- Where are procurement, payment, revenue, audit, or grant compliance bottlenecks?
- Are residents and businesses able to make payments or receive services efficiently?
- Which fiscal risks need enterprise attention?

### Starter Focus Areas

- Procurement and purchasing queue visibility
- Audit findings and grant compliance accountability
- Online payments, collections, and contract data integration

### Standard Artifacts and Data Products

- Pre-meeting memo, Stat deck, follow-up memo, commitment tracker, and decision log.
- Core data product: Financial operations and compliance dashboard.
- Public summary, annual report section, data quality notes, and SOP / process maps for major functions.
- Inspection checklist where field validation or service quality review is needed.

### Tiger Team / Delivery Triggers

- Procurement or fiscal barrier affecting multiple agencies
- Audit or grant compliance issue requiring executive action
- Financial data integration issue requiring Data / Tech support
