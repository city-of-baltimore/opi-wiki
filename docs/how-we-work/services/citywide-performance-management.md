# Citywide Performance Management

> The executive performance rhythm that turns priorities into measures, commitments, and follow-through.

Citywide Performance Management is the service delivered by the [Performance team](../../our-teams/performance/index.md). It is how Baltimore sees performance clearly, asks better questions, assigns commitments, and tracks them to closure.

## What it does

The service gives the City a disciplined rhythm for management attention: priorities become measures, leaders see what is working and what is stuck, and commitments turn into measurable service improvements. Its day-to-day expression is the [CitiStat program](../../programs/citistat/index.md) — the recurring, citywide routine the Performance team operates and all of OPI supports.

## Why it matters

In the signal-to-solution loop, this is the step that **identifies the problem**: CitiStat brings agencies, OPI, and City leadership together around data, barriers, and commitments, and decides what needs to happen next.
