**Subject:**  
Guidance on Media and Press Engagement

As we work toward making the OPI brand more visible, I want to clarify
expectations regarding engagement with members of the press and media outlets.

OPI staff should not engage directly with reporters, media outlets, newsletter
writers, podcast hosts, or other media representatives regarding official city
business without prior coordination with the Mayor's Office of Communications.

This includes:

- Responding to interview requests

- Providing comments or quotes

- Participating in podcasts, newsletters, or public-facing publications

- Discussing official city initiatives, data, policies, or projects

- Responding to media inquiries received via email, social media, or phone

If you are contacted by a member of the media, please do not respond
substantively. Instead, promptly forward the request to the Mayor's Office of
Communications and notify your supervisor.

The Communications team is responsible for coordinating messaging, ensuring
accuracy, and aligning external communications with Administration priorities.

This guidance is not intended to discourage thought leadership or professional
engagement, but rather to ensure that all official communications are
coordinated, accurate, and consistent.

If you are unsure whether something qualifies as media engagement, elevate the
question before responding.

Thank you for your attention to this standard.
