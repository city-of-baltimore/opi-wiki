# How to Edit a Past Reporting Period in Book of Business

{{ page_header() }}

Follow these steps to locate and edit a past reporting period in the Book of
Business list.

A video walkthrough is embedded below.

## Step 1: Navigate to Past Periods

In the left navigation, select **Book of Business**, then **Weekly Update**.

The list will automatically load to active period.

Ensure you are in the `Past Periods` view at the top of the list.

## Step 2: Filter to Your Initiative or Name

Use the filter options to narrow results:

- Filter by your Initiative name

- OR filter by the `Lead` column and select your name

This helps quickly find the correct entry.

## Step 3: Locate the Item

Scroll or search within the filtered list to find the reporting period you want
to edit.

## Step 4: Edit the Item (Quick Method)

Double-click the cell, switch to **Edit in grid view**, or click directly into a field.

Make your updates and ensure changes are saved.

## Step 5: Edit Using Full Panel (Recommended for Multiple Fields)

Click the Title of the item.

A panel will open on the right.

Click `Edit`, update fields, then click `Save`.

## Troubleshooting

- If fields are not editable, they may be locked or system-managed.

- Check your permissions if editing is restricted.

- Try switching to `All Items` view.

- Use `Edit in grid view` for bulk or alternative editing.

- If needed, duplicate the item and update the new version.

Please also reach out to the Director's Office if you have any questions, or
need further troubleshooting.
