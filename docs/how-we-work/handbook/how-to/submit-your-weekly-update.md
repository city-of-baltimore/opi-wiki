# Submit Your Weekly Book of Business Update

{{ page_header() }}

Welcome to the SharePoint Book of Business (BoB), our centralized way of
tracking progress, priorities, and key updates across requests, operations,
products, programs, projects, and stats.

Goal: To provide clear, consistent visibility into what's moving forward, what's
at risk, and where support or decisions are needed.

Due: Every Thursday by COB

Responsibility: Any initiatives where you are listed as the lead of that
initiative.

Link: Book of Business - Weekly Updates

Mallory Screws (Project Manager) is your primary point of contact for the BoB. If you have questions,
need help, or want to walk through your updates together, feel free to reach out
or set up time with her.

## How to Locate the BoB

1. Navigate to our SharePoint (SP) site Home

2. Go to Book of Business - Weekly Updates

3. Open the BoB list to view all initiatives

Pro tip: Filter the list to only show your initiatives that you lead.

## How to Update Your Initiatives (Grid View)

1. Select `Edit in grid view` at the top of the list

2. Find the initiative(s) where you are listed as the leader

3. Update the relevant fields directly in the grid

4. Save each entry by clicking the check mark in the text box.

Pro tip: Exit grid view once complete (changes will auto-save).

## How to Update Your Initiatives (Full Item Panel)

- Double Click the Title of the entry.

- This panel will open.

- Select Edit all at the top.

- When finished hit Submit.

## Ownership Reminder

You are responsible for updating any initiatives where you are listed as the
leader of the initiative.

## How to Structure Your Updates

| **Section** | **What to Include** | **Guiding Questions** | **Formatting Rules** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Progress | What moved forward or was completed | What changed since last week? What is now done or unblocked? | Focus on outcomes, not activity |
| Highlights | Most important wins | What matters most to leadership? What moved us forward? | Short and scannable |
| Lowlights | Delays or challenges | What is slower, incomplete, or not ideal? | Keep it brief and clear |
| Blockers | What is preventing progress | What is stuck? What is needed and from whom? | Include clear ask + owner |
| Next Steps | Immediate upcoming work | What will get done next (1-2 weeks)? | Keep actionable |
| Decisions Needed | Initiatives requiring direction | What needs a clear yes/no or guidance? | Phrase as a decision |

## Additional Tips

- Focus on outcomes rather than activity

- Clearly call out blockers and decisions, these are the most critical

- Avoid vague updates like `working on...`; say specifically what changed.
