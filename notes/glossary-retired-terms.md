# Glossary — retired and use-carefully terms (preserved)

This note preserves the **Terms to use carefully** and **Retired or use-carefully terms** sections that were removed from `docs/resources/reference/glossary.md` in the CitiStat process-pass deepening PR (April 2026). They were removed from the public glossary at the Executive Director's direction so the published reference shows OPI's active vocabulary only.

The text below is preserved verbatim so editors can recover historical guidance, retire-decisions, and replacement-term reasoning if questions come up later.

---

## ■ Terms to use carefully (verbatim, removed April 2026)

**Baltimore Design Lab.** Use this term only to describe OPI's design community of practice or a design sub-capability connected to the Innovation Lab. Do not use it as a separate portfolio, service, or office unless leadership formally approves that structure.

**Citywide Digital Services.** Use this term only when referring to digital services brand stewardship or citywide digital service request routing. Do not use it as a replacement for the Innovation Lab, which is the canonical portfolio and service name for OPI's applied innovation, design, product, civic technology, and AI pilot work.

**Delivery room.** Avoid this term unless a specific engagement has formally adopted it. Prefer "delivery review," "delivery cadence," or "delivery activation," depending on whether the sentence is describing the forum, the rhythm, or the decision to activate Cross-Agency Delivery.

**Joint Stat.** Use only when a CitiStat session intentionally brings two or more agencies into the same performance routine for a shared problem. A Joint Stat makes decisions and commitments. A delivery review runs implementation between Stats.

**Signal-to-solution loop.** OPI shorthand for how work moves from performance signal to sustained improvement: CitiStat identifies the problem; Data helps explain it; the Innovation Lab designs and tests the solution; Cross-Agency Delivery coordinates implementation when multiple agencies must act together; AdminOps keeps the work visible, documented, and sustained.

## ■ Retired or use-carefully terms (verbatim, removed April 2026)

**Data and Digital.** Historical or transitional phrasing only. Use **Data and Analytics** for the current portfolio and service unless quoting or describing older material.

**Citywide Digital Services.** Use carefully. Do not use as a standing team or portfolio name. Prefer **Innovation Lab** for the portfolio and use "citywide digital services request" only when describing request routing or brand stewardship.

**Baltimore Design Lab.** Use as a design community of practice or sub-capability, not as a separate OPI portfolio or service unless formally approved.

**Delivery room.** Avoid unless a specific routine is formally named that way. Prefer **delivery review**, **delivery cadence**, or **delivery activation**.

**Joint Stat.** Use only when a CitiStat routine intentionally brings multiple agencies into one performance conversation for a shared problem. Do not use it as a synonym for a delivery review. A Joint Stat decides on performance and commitments; a delivery review manages implementation between decision points.

**Admin Ops.** Retired spelling. Use **AdminOps**.
